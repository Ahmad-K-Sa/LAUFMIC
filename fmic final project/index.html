Hult Program

• Hult Prize is one of the biggest yearly global competitions for university students that develops their social entrepreneurship skills.

• The Hult Prize aims to find solutions for the world's most pressing social challenges, through encouraging university students to invent their own solutions, thus get pushed into the world of startups and entrepreneurship.

Total Startupper Challenge

StartUpper of the Year Challenge by Total supports and rewards young local entrepreneurs in any business sector with a project or business less than two years old.

•Start-Upper project history:
- Launch: 2015 in more than 30 African countries.
- 2018’s reach: 60 countries worldwide & Lebanon taking part for the first time.

Tech Sprint

In Collaboration with UK Lebanon Tech Hub, the Sprint workshop consists of:
• Identifying stakeholders & their challenges.
• Ideation Session.
• Lean Startup Canvas & pitching of ideas.
• Validation through interviews, cost structure, marketing & comparative analysis as well as open discussions to solve.
